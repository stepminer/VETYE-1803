Who We Are

VÈTYÈ-1803 is a global movement inspired by the bravery and unity of the Haitian ancestors who secured victory on November 18, 1803. We unite Haitians worldwide to create a sovereign, equitable, and thriving Haiti.

Our Actions

Our mission is to empower Haitian communities through:

  • Grassroots Organization: Building strong local groups to defend rights and resources.
  • Support for Resistance: Offering logistics, funding, and advocacy for transformative efforts.
  • Unity Building: Bridging Haitians within and outside the country toward shared goals.

Our Results

VÈTYÈ-1803 is proud to:

  • Provide IDP families with access to clean water.
  • Empower grassroots organizations with training and resources.
  • Amplify Diaspora voices in Haitian governance.
